Welcome to Scribe

Located on historic Filbert Street, Scribe is a boutique printing and stationery atelier dedicated to the art of fine paper goods. We specialize in custom wedding invitations, event stationery, letterpress printing, and bespoke correspondence that reflects your unique voice and style.

Each piece is thoughtfully designed and crafted with traditional techniques and the finest materials—because the details matter, and your story deserves to be told beautifully.
