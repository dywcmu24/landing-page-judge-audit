A Philadelphia Institution

Since 1974, Maser Galleries has been a cornerstone of Philadelphia's vibrant art community on historic Walnut Street. For nearly five decades, we have curated exceptional collections and provided museum-quality custom framing services to discerning collectors, institutions, and artists.

Our gallery represents both established and emerging artists across diverse media, from contemporary paintings and sculpture to photography and works on paper. Each piece in our collection is carefully selected to reflect our commitment to artistic excellence and cultural significance.

Our conservation-grade custom framing studio employs master framers with decades of experience, utilizing archival materials and techniques to preserve and enhance your most treasured artworks and heirlooms.

Our Services

Fine Art Gallery

Art Consultation

Expert guidance for private collectors and corporate clients. We assist with acquisitions, collection development, and installation services.
