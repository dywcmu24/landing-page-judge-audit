Welcome to Our Little Corner of Shadyside

For over 55 years, Kards Unlimited has been the heart of Shadyside's Walnut Street, tucked between the coffee shops and boutiques you love. We're not just a card shop—we're the place where you find that perfect something that makes someone's day.

Step inside and you'll discover shelves brimming with quirky greeting cards, thoughtfully curated gifts, engaging books, and charming stationery. Every item in our shop is hand-picked with love, because we believe that the small details matter most.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, saying thank you, or just looking for a little pick-me-up, we're here to help you find exactly what you didn't know you were looking for. That's the Kards Unlimited magic.
