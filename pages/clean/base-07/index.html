Your Neighborhood Treasure Trove

Shadyside Variety Store is a whimsical toy and novelty gift shop nestled right in the heart of Shadyside at Walnut & Copeland. We're not just a store—we're a portal back to the pure delight of discovering something unexpected and wonderful.

From classic toys that bring back your childhood memories to quirky gifts that make everyone giggle, our carefully curated collection celebrates the playful spirit in all of us. Whether you're hunting for the perfect birthday present, a unique conversation starter, or just want to treat yourself to something delightful, we've got shelves packed with possibilities.

Every corner is bursting with colorful surprises: vintage-inspired wind-up toys, puzzles that challenge and delight, whimsical greeting cards, nostalgic candy, silly putty, magic tricks, and novelties you won't find anywhere else. Come get lost in the fun!
