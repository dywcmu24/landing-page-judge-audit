A Sanctuary for the Bibliophile & Audiophile

Amazing Books & Records is Shadyside's premier destination for thoughtfully selected used books and vintage vinyl records. Since opening our doors, we've cultivated a collection that prizes quality over quantity, character over condition, and discovery over mere transaction.

Our shop is a refuge from the algorithm—a place where serendipity reigns and every visit yields unexpected treasures. Whether you're searching for a first edition of a beloved classic, an obscure philosophy text, or that elusive pressing of a jazz masterwork, our shelves hold the promise of literary and sonic revelation.

We specialize in carefully chosen literature, humanities, art books, and records spanning jazz, classical, folk, and indie rock. Each item has been selected by our knowledgeable staff who share your passion for the tactile, the analog, and the enduring.

Browse by Genre

Literary Fiction
From modernist masterworks to contemporary voices, our literary fiction section offers carefully selected novels that challenge, illuminate, and endure. Discover Woolf alongside Wallace, Faulkner beside Ferrante—each spine a doorway to another consciousness.
Philosophy & Critical Theory
Engage with the thinkers who've shaped our understanding of existence, society, and meaning. Our collection spans from ancient Stoics to postmodern theorists, featuring well-loved editions with marginalia that traces previous readers' intellectual journeys.
Art & Photography
Museum-quality art books, monographs, and photography collections at accessible prices. Whether you seek inspiration, scholarship, or simply beautiful objects, our curated selection celebrates visual culture across movements and media.
Jazz & Blues Vinyl
Exceptional pressings of the American art form. From bebop innovators to modal explorers, our jazz collection includes rare imports, original Blue Note releases, and essential recordings that belong in every serious collection. Each record graded honestly and priced fairly.
Classical & Orchestral
Historic performances from legendary conductors and soloists. Our classical section features Deutsche Grammophon, Decca, and RCA Living Stereo pressings that capture the warmth and dynamics digital formats struggle to replicate.
Folk & Singer-Songwriter
Intimate voices and acoustic authenticity. From Greenwich Village folk revival to contemporary indie folk, these records capture the tradition of storytelling through song. Perfect for late-night listening and contemplative moments.
